Our Story so far

Tenkun Consulting Associates was incorporated in Bangalore as its Head office.. The company has since grown to be a leading provider of talent management solutions with service offerings that include HR Management Consulting, Executive Search, Executive Selection, Staff Leasing, Recruitment Process Outsourcing and Learning and Development. Our "The Best Fit" methodology has attracted many clients, which include MNCs from various industries. TCA believes in the motto "simplification" and hence the company tag line Consulting.Learning..Staffing..Simplified. TCA believes in simplifying the practices for client to seamlessly deliver solutions to their requirements. With our dedicated approach, our distinctive on-demand, cost-efficient and integrated approach has enabled us to achieve a very good reputation amongst our clients. Our result-oriented approach has also helped us earn nearly 100% customer referrals.

Our Approach

We focus on detailed understanding of client's needs and coordinate periodically to develop the best in class solutions. In the process of helping our organizations in achieving the goals in most efficient and cost effective manner, our services are competitively priced and are aligned to the Industry standards. Through our well designed processes and structured methodology, we guarantee best results in most limited time span. Our offerings are flexible and cater to any problem as specified by the clients. We have a proven track record of meeting the most complex talent needs of clients Our continuous endeavor is to always exceed expectations and provide best in class solutions. Customer satisfaction is the core essence of all our initiatives.

Our Strengths

Tenkun Consulting specializes in helping clients quickly within defined turn-around time to cost-effectively meet their hiring needs for various levels across all management disciplines & across industries with quality professionals on a permanent as well as contract basis. Tenkun Consulting supports clients with round-the-clock solutions in talent management solutions covering all the core management disciplines across a multitude of industry domains. Our team of experts utilizes human resources management experience to fulfil your unique business requirements. All of our consultants have broad experience and extensive knowledge within all phases of Infrastructure, Road, Rail, Highways, Telecommunications, Healthcare,Finance, Insurance, FMCG, Pharmaceuticals, Power, Construction, Manufacturing, Service, Retailing, Trade and Shared Services.
