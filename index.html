About TCA

Tenkun Consulting Associates was incorporated in Bangalore as its Head office. The company has since grown to be a leading provider of talent management solutions with service offerings that include HR Management Consulting, Executive Search, Executive Selection, Staff Leasing, Recruitment Process Outsourcing and Learning and Development. Our "The Best Fit" methodology has attracted many clients, which include MNCs from various industries.
